How payment works

  1. Choose a plan when you create a case — one-off or subscription.
  2. Connect a wallet from the sidebar if prompted (Smart Account is optional in demos).
  3. x402 session is prepared and bound to your case via scoped ERC-7710 permission.
  4. Agent AI unlocks once the session is authorized or paid — approvals still required for every send.

FAQ

Can I switch from one-off to subscription later?

Yes. After a one-off run, open Settings → Payment rails or use the upgrade prompt on your case overview.

How is Oblivion different from Incogni or DeleteMe?

Broker services run opt-outs on their schedule and hold your data in their systems. Oblivion keeps sensitive identifiers in your browser vault, charges per agent capacity in USDC, and pauses for your approval before anything is sent.

Does paying skip the approval step?

No. Payment only funds agent assistance. Every disclosure still goes through propose → approve → execute.

What chain and token do you use?

USDC on Base via x402. Demo mode can auto-authorize sessions; live deployments settle through your connected wallet.

Do I need a subscription to paste my own URLs?

No. Discovery and review work on both plans. Subscription adds monitoring cadence and higher AI limits.

When should I use the free SKILL.md instead?

Use the skill when you already run Cursor, Claude, Codex, or Grok locally and want the supervised cleanup playbook without the Oblivion UI or x402 payments. You bring the LLM; Oblivion managed plans add discovery, monitoring, and capped in-app agent assistance.
